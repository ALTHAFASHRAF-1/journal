Call for Papers

Islamic Insight Journal of Islamic Studies

Upcoming Issue: Vol. 8, No. 1 (2025)

We are pleased to invite original research articles for the upcoming issue of Islamic Insight, a biannual peer-reviewed journal published by the Kulliyyah of Qur'an and Sunnah, Darul Huda Islamic University, Kerala, India.

Islamic Insight welcomes high-quality, interdisciplinary research in areas related to Islamic revealed knowledge and the Muslim world. This includes—but is not limited to—studies in the Noble Qur'an, Hadith, Islamic Theology, Islamic Social Sciences, and Muslim History and Culture.

Important Deadline

Submit your manuscripts by mid-June 2025 for consideration in Vol. 8, No. 1 (2025).

Scope & Research Areas

We welcome submissions across various disciplines within Islamic Studies:

Quranic Studies

Exegesis, textual analysis, thematic studies, and contemporary interpretations

Hadith Scholarship

Hadith criticism, authentication methods, and prophetic traditions

Islamic Theology

Aqeedah, Kalam, theological debates, and comparative theology

Islamic Law & Jurisprudence

Fiqh, legal theory, contemporary legal issues, and comparative law

Islamic History & Civilization

Historical analysis, cultural studies, and civilizational contributions

Contemporary Islamic Thought

Modern Islamic movements, reform, and contemporary challenges

Unique and relevant contributions beyond the prescribed disciplines are also encouraged.

Submission Guidelines

  • Original Research: All submissions must be original, unpublished work not under consideration elsewhere.
  • Language: Manuscripts may be submitted in English or Arabic.
  • Peer Review: All submissions undergo a rigorous double-blind peer review process.
  • Word Limit: Research articles should be between 6,000-8,000 words, including references.
  • Format: Follow standard academic formatting with proper citations and bibliography.
  • Abstract: Include a 200-250 word abstract with 5-7 keywords.
  • Biography: Provide a brief author biography (100-150 words).
  • File Format: Submit manuscripts in Microsoft Word (.docx) format.

Review Process Timeline

Initial Submission

Authors submit manuscripts via email

Editorial Review

Initial assessment within 2-3 weeks

Peer Review

Double-blind review process (6-8 weeks)

Final Decision

Authors notified of acceptance/revision/rejection
